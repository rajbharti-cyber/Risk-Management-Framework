## 📌 Purpose

This template demonstrates how cyber and technology risks can be documented, tracked, and governed within a structured risk management process.

---

## 🎯 Key Components

### Risk Identification
Defines the specific technology or security risk.

### Business Impact
Describes operational, regulatory, financial, or reputational impact.

### Existing Controls
Documents preventive and detective controls currently implemented.

### Residual Risk
Represents remaining risk after controls are considered.

### Remediation Tracking
Tracks mitigation plans and governance actions.
